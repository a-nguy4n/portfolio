Bio Overview

If I’m not busy, something probably feels off. I am currently a computer engineering student with a minor in design, curious about how things go from a simple idea to something people actually enjoy using (or don’t).


Lately, I've been working on projects that let me experiment with both development and design, refining my skills and trying new things as I go. Outside of that, I’m usually out on a trail, trying a new coffee spot, or convincing myself I can pick up a new skill, lately pottery.
